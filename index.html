The Opportunity

Poland is Europe's fastest-growing tech ecosystem and the leader in CEE startup value.

The country's deep tech scene is booming: robotics ranked 5th in Europe, €800M in VC funding deployed in 2025 alone, and global success stories from ElevenLabs to ICEYE proving that Polish engineering can compete at the highest level. Yet the most defensible early-stage technologies remain invisible to international investors. Finding them requires deep local access that most funds simply don't have.
